Who am I?

My journey as an artist started when I was a just child. Attending painting classes in my early teens and inspirations by cartoons, video games and Japanese pop culture finally brought my passion for art and design to life. Since then I've been working on private projects as well as commission work for classmates, friends and accquaintances.

My focus used to be on illustrations only, but I wasn't satisfied with that. Therefore, the wish for a broader skillset motivates me to develop and improve my graphic design and visual communication skills, which is initially one of many reasons why I want to become a graphic designer.
